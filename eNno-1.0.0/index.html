About The Paramount Medical Center

The Paramount Medical Center (TPMC) is a level 2 Hospital, Modern and Smart, Green and Efficient, and User-friendly with Multi-specialty doctors. We’re an innovative healthcare provider focused on meeting the diverse needs of our patients, including urgent care, primary care, specialty services, and preventive health—all under one roof.
